Marges Lunch

Description

Delicious and easy to make, this will become one of your staples!

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat
  • 3 carrots
  • 10 potatos
  • 1 onion
  • 1tbs oil

Process

  1. Invite guests
  2. Fry onion on pan
  3. Add meat to pan, fry until golden
  4. Chop vegetables, boil until soft
  5. Mix meat and vegetables together and let simmer
